Hi, I'm Ben. I recently completed a PhD in Quantum Information Theory at the University of Bristol.

Beyond academia, I have experience consulting in the quantum computing industry, including work with Phasecraft.

I also offer freelance consulting and private tutoring in mathematics and music.

I'm always keen to collaborate on interesting projects and open to new opportunities—feel free to get in touch!

Interests

My research has focused on quantum resource theories, measurement incompatibility, quantum algorithms, and quantum entanglement.

Quantum information is a fascinating field that blends the counterintuitive principles of quantum mechanics with deep ideas from computer science, such as how to quantify information and what it means to compute efficiently.

I enjoy collaborating on conceptual problems where rigorous mathematical statements can be made, and implementing them in code.

I'm particularly interested in:

  • Quantum computing: algorithms, error correction, and quantum resource theories
  • Mathematical foundations of quantum mechanics: measurement incompatibility and entanglement
  • Software development: writing clean, efficient code for research and applications
  • Artificial intelligence: both understanding AI research and integrating AI tools into scientific workflows

Bio

I grew up in Rotherham, in the north of England. I did a Maths undergrad and masters in Durham, UK, with a year abroad in Switzerland taking courses taught in French. I then worked in a Computer Science department for 9 months before interning at a quantum startup in Singapore. Following this, I began my PhD studies in Bristol, during which I spent several months visiting the University of Geneva, as well as interning at Phasecraft. I took a career break post PhD doing some travel, freelance consulting, and tutoring (mostly mathematics and music lessons, to students of different ages). Outside of work I love to play sports, create music, and learn languages. Some causes that I care about include mental health, education, and homelessness.
